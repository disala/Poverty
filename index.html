Poverty profile of Sri Lanka

Department of Census and Statistics, Sri Lanka

Department of census and statistics published range of publications related to the poverty over its long history. In 1990 department started conducting HIES survey with the intention of providing wide range of poverty related statistics catering local and international requirements to eradicate the poverty in Sri Lanka. The poverty profile of Sri Lanka collects all vital Statistics and related publications for the first time in the history in line with government intention to declare 2017 as the poverty eradication year for Sri Lanka.

This supplementary Compact Disk attached to the “Poverty Profile of Sri Lanka” consists with e-versions of all related tables, charts, maps and publications which are available in the publication. The content of this CD is arranged according to the chapters of the report for your easy reference.
